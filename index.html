Aizoban

An Aizōban is a quality collector's edition volume. Likewise, "Aizoban: Your Manga Reader" is an online and an offline manga reader which aims to provide a great visual user experience when browsing and reading manga.

✔ Access to over 35 000 manga series from currently 5 sources: MangaEden (EN), MangaHere (EN), MangaReader (EN), MangaEden (IT), and MangaHere (ES).
✔ Amazingly fast and immersing viewer with options to switch reading direction, lock orientation, and lock zoom.
✔ A search tool capable of instantly filtering and ordering manga series by name, genre (not for all sources), and status.
✔ An "Exploring" function to discover random manga series.
✔ A download manager to collect your desired manga series offline.
✔ Your personalized list of favourite manga series and recently read chapters.
✔ Easy settings to manage your reading experience such as "Enable Lazy Loading" to conserve bandwidth.
